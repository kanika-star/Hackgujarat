Different Streams available after class10-

SCIENCE

The subjects studied in this course are Physics,Maths,Chemistry,Biology ,Computer along with English and a vernacular language ,depending on the board and mediumyou opt for.
Science is the most sought after the course and one that almost every parent wants their child to take up as this stream has a number of regarding career opportunities including engineering , medical,and research in different domains.
It allows students to change streams and take up arts or commerce later in their lives if they are not in ease.

COMMERCE

Best of business,commerce is known as the second most popular stream.If you are fascinated by numbers,finances,and economics ,commerce is for you.The stream has promising and handsomely payingprofessions like chartered accountant ,company secretaries,accountants,investment banking and financial advisors.However one has to enroll for professional courses in the respective domains after completing class12.These include B.com,BBA,BMS,BBM,CFA,CA,ICWA,CFP ETC.
Subjects include Business Economics,Accountancy,Business study and business law.Besides,basic knowledge of accounting ,income tax,marketing.

ARTS

Humanities or Arts hitherto known as the least preferred stream and limited to students who were interested in academic research ,is now emerging as a sought after career choice as today there are opportunities galore in this domain including some interesting ,off-beat and exciting career options.Arts now offer an array of career alternatives which are equally rewarding and fulfilling as the ones offered by science or commerce streams.
One can opt for career paths in Mass Media,Journalism,Literature,Languages,Social Service.

Career guidance after class12 standard-

Chartered Accountancy

Chartered Accountancy is one of the most sought career options only not among commerce stream but science stream as well.
This course makes you learn how to prepare and handle financial affairs of the company .They play an important role in the growth of an organization as well as the nation building.The profile is to look after the taxation,financial,accounting and auditing matters.
Anyone after class12 can registered for CA course by qualifying its entrance level test named as CA Foundation.

Engineering

Every year over 10 lacs students appear in JEE Mains exams,but only a few clear the exam.This has been referred to as the toughest exam in our country.Science stream students other than having biology mainly choose to do engineering,but you wiil be shocked to know that there are many other career options CA after 12th for science students
The students pertaining interest in software designing ,software programming,or even hardware integration made for engineering.
B.Tech is a 4-year programme or a graduation course.

Medical Profession

If biology were your subject and you have a deep interest in such then you have an option to choose from many trending medical courses right after 12th.
Well,the most sought option is AIPMT,an entrance test for MBBS but very few are able to crack this exam.Thereafter,the most happening medical course is pharmacology where the use of drugs has been guided to students.From this course,they would able to learn the drug immunity.Its effects ,side effects of multiple components on the patient.

Fashion Designing

Most popular course among the arts stream students.The people who are blesses with the creative minds can opt for this course.The students after class 12th can go for some fashion designing diploma course and build their career in the most creative and influencing field.
The most influencing part of this course is that you can either go for service or start your own boutique at any residing place even at your home also.

B.Com(Bachelor of Commerce)

The most sought course among the commerce stream students after class 12th .Even the students opted for any professional courses like CA,CS,CMA,ACCA etc also pursue B.Com to obtain a side degree.There are many reasons why one go with B.Com.
B.Com is a three years graduation program which can commence right after 12th.After completing B.Com ,one may go with M.Com which will take a minimum of two years to complete.
Main advantage of doing B.Com is that it opens the door to pursue many govt. courses like IAS,IPS,UPSC etc.Another advantage is that one can pursue MBA or other post graduation course after completing his B.Com.

BBA(Bachelor of Business Administration)

BBA is a Bachelor's degree in business administration.Students who are preparing for MBA should start their career with BBA after 12th.It is a three years graduation program and thereafter follows it with two years MBA program.
It lands you numberless jobs at managerial post in the private sector and government sector both.However ,for BBA's, the private sector is more fruitful as every organization needs an MBA to effectively manage his business.Those who are looking for a career in management can go with BBA.

CS(Company Secretary)

You must have heard about CS earlier,professionals who are known to handle the corporate governance of the company.This course has been offered by the ICSE(Institute of company secretaries of India).The duration of this professional course is minimum three years divided into three stages similar to CA and CMA.The entrance test is the CS Foundation,thereafter CS Executive and last CS Professional.
One can enroll for CS Foundation after qualifying 10+2 from a recognized board of education.
The average earnings of a CS range between 3-5 lakhs.
